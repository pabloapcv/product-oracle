# Risk & Compliance

## Scraping / Data Collection
- Respect robots.txt where applicable
- Rate limit requests
- Cache responses
- Keep raw logs for audit

## Product Risk Flags (Do Not Recommend / High Caution)
- Supplements / medical claims
- Kids safety-critical items
- High hazard materials
- Counterfeit/IP-heavy categories
- Batteries without compliance pathway

## Output Warnings
Every opportunity should include:
- Regulatory risk
- Return risk proxies
- IP/copy risk proxy
